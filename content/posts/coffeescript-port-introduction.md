To test out the claims made by CoffeeScript, we are going to port the Sylvester open source JavaScript library.  It is a library which includes objects for modeling vectors matrices, lines, and planes in 3D space.  At first we will focus on the Vector object, and then move to the Matrix, Line, and Plane objects.
